Professional Bio

My name is Jawad, and I am a content creator and vlogger from Rayachoty. Through my videos, I focus on real-life stories, local culture, architecture, travel, and the voices of common people.

I believe every place has a story, and every person has a voice that deserves to be heard. My content is grounded, honest, and close to reality — no fake drama, no artificial trends.

The Digital Diary

"Main Jawad hoon, Rayachoty se. Mera kaam sirf vlogging nahi, balki haqeeqat ko camera me qaid karna hai."

From street-level vlogs to cultural events, from heritage locations to public issues, I try to document everything with truth and respect. Its Me Jawad is not just a YouTube channel; it’s a digital diary of Rayachoty and beyond.

Logon ki zindagi, hamari tehzeeb, jagah aur awaaz — sab kuch asli, bina banawat. Agar aap real stories aur authentic visuals pasand karte hain, toh aap sahi jagah par hain.
